About the ESAT

The Engineering and Science Admissions Test (ESAT) is a new admissions assessment first administered in October 2024. It is used by top UK universities including Cambridge and Imperial College London for applicants to engineering and science programs.

Test Structure

Mathematics 1 Mathematics 2 Physics Chemistry Biology
Scoring

Scored on a scale of 1-9

Test Dates

Administered in October and January

First Administered

October 2024
